Blockle

Have you ever had arguments with yourself? Do you consider yourself the smartest in the room? Well, everything in life is perspective. We often forget there are always two (multiple) sides to the same coin. Proteus is back with another thrilling event to bring out perspective in a humorous way. Here is an opportunity to voice out your thoughts on two opposing sides simultaneously! Ever thought about how fun it'd be to defend and attack the same topic... Yes, Block and Tackle, a fast-paced game that forces you to do just that. We're back with our second event of Rhetor 2.0, Blockle. Get ready to take a trip on a ferris wheel of excitement, and display your impromptu speaking prowess to win exciting cash prizes! Register here!

Rules and Regulations

  1. Block-To defend the topic
  2. Tackle-Oppose the topic
  3. Participants will have to speak for and against a given topic on the judge’s call.
  4. Participants will be provided with a limited amount of preparation time
  5. Speaking Time: 1 minute 30 second
  6. Judging criteria will be based on the content, transitions, and spontaneity exhibited during transitions.
